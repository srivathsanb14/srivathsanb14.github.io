About Me

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, where I will continue my work in the Mechanical and AI Lab with Dr. Amir Barati Farimani. My research focuses on physics-informed AI that unifies molecular language, graph, and 3D structure for therapeutic and materials discovery in data-limited settings.

I recently completed an M.S. in Artificial Intelligence Engineering (Chemical Engineering) at CMU. Before that, I earned a B.Tech in Chemical Engineering from IIT Madras, with research experience spanning MAIL Lab, MITACS at the University of Alberta, and IIT Madras. You can find more about my publications and projects and a copy of my CV.
